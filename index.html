Programming has thousands of new terms and jargon

  • git: A versioning system.
  • rake: A tool for running tasks)
  • database: A place to store lots of data.
  • editor: KomodoEdit or your favorite text editor.
  • heroku: A place to host your Rails website.
  • DRY: Don't Repeat Yourself.
  • Convention Over Configuration
  • And way way way more.

You're teachers will use jargon without even knowing it. Interupt them.
